BEAUTY tip...

So my beauties for the past year I have been growing my eyelashes with a homemade DIY oil and I just had to share this with you guys as it has made my eyelashes super strong and healthy and I have definitely noticed growth too!

Here is my recipe:
1 tbsp castor oil
1 tbsp coconut oil
1 tbsp sweet almond oil
1 capsule of vitamin E oil

Castor oil is often used to treat baldness and prevent hair fall. It’s thick oil which lubricates the hair follicles well and allows growth and strengthening. It’s rich in Vitamin E, protein and many fatty acids.

Coconut oil is used for everything be it skin, hair or cooking. It’s widely available and used by people. Being Indian I was told about the benefits of coconut oil from a young age. It boosts hair growth as it contains fatty acids which can easily penetrate into the hair.

Sweet almond oil is strengthening and moisturizing to the hair. It’s rich in monosaturated fats, Vitamin E, calcium and many essential fatty acids.

Vitamin E oil is the ultimate beauty oil it’s great for our skin and hair. It strengthens hair and prevents hair loss.

I used this oil every night; I simply applied it to my lashes using a spoolie brush! Give it a go my angels and let me know what you guys think!
