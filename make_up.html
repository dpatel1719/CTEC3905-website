UNDERRATED PALETTE

FEB 2017

I’m talking about none other than the...

Zoeva Mixed Metals palette.

Zoeva is better known for their makeup brushes, they are some of my favourite makeup brushes. Therefore when Zoeva came out with this eyeshadow palette I was a bit sceptical at first to give it a try but I had to test out what all the hype was about. The palette has 10 beautiful shades all but the ‘Onyx’ shade are stunningly shimmery. Although the Onyx shade is absolutely great to create a smokey eye as its a really dark intense black shade. All the eyeshadows are super pigmented so a little goes a long way, one dab on the brush is more than enough for the whole lid. I LOVE the ‘Warm Silver’ shade its such a gorgeous shade to use as a highlight. I often use that to highlight the brows and inner corners of the eye. Its a great travel palette very light and compact and covers a superb range of colours so you do not have to carry extra eyeshadows. Great quality and affordable its a winner in my books!

The Zoeva Mixed Metals Palette is £17.50 & available HERE

GLITTER MAKES EVERYTHING BETTER

JAN 2017

The Urban Decay Heavy Metal Glitter Eyeliners are soooooo pretty!

I bought the ‘Spandex’ shade first as I loved the colour, its such a beautiful midnight blue shade I just could not resist. When I tried out the eyeliner I was in love! I just had to go and buy all the shades. The eyeliner is very fine and the right consistency to spread evenly, and with every swipe you get such intense glitter pigmentation it’s unreal! I have not owned a glittery eyeliner like that before. I have been using it on the lid as a eyeshadow too and it looks so beautiful and sparkly and it’s great on camera its not too flashy or in your face it’s just the right amount of sparkle. Truly OBSESSED with these right now absolutely loving glitter at the moment and these defiantly are a must have for any glitter fanatic like me!

The Urban Decay Heavy Metal glitter eyeliners are £15 & available HERE
