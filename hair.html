I have super thick and long hair and many hair straighteners I have tried in the past may have straightened my hair but left it frizzy and dry.

However one day I was casually scrolling across my newsfeed on Facebook and I came across these Glamoriser hair straighteners so I read the comments and all of them were great but I thought no I have my GHDs which work great already. So when I received them I had to test them out straight away! Super easy to turn on and use instantly. They heat up in less than 15 seconds and it makes a beep noise when they are ready for use. HAIR routine...

So recently I have changed up my hair routine as I have been experiencing a lot of hair fall. Oil treatments I have been using for many years, I apply oil once a week and as you guys know I LOVE my coconut oil!
I recently came across ‘L’Oreal Elvive Low Shampoo’. It’s a sulphate free shampoo so like your regular shampoo it does not foam up which at first feels weird I must say. It’s like a cleansing cream for the hair. Its texture is like hair conditioner and with this shampoo you do not need to use a separate conditioner which saves so much time and money I think.
The actual shampoo can be difficult to work with and apply as it does not lather but after a couple of uses you grasp the concept. But the main reason I have decided to write about this is because it has left my hair so nourished and smooth. My hair feels and looks healthy and stronger.
I am really impressed with this new shampoo. However it can be a bit on the expensive side as you require a good 5-6 pumps of the shampoo for one use. I need that much to cover my long thick hair but if you have shorter hair 2-3 pumps is adequate. The shampoo price ranges from £5-7 so it would be good to get a couple of bottles when on offer!
But overall guys give it a try it’s a different experience but leaves your hair very healthy and shiny. It’s one of those hit or miss products with people but give it a try and let me know what you guys think!
